A Story for You

Aníké

Here is a journey that spans cities, but always leads to one place...

Chapter I: The Arrival

"From Lagos streets where the city never sleeps,
He carried noise, ambition, and restless dreams,
Till Ibadan slowed his hurried heartbeat down
The day he met her, love arrived quietly, it seems."

Chapter II: The Peace

"In her laughter he found a gentler home,
In her eyes, a peace he never knew he sought,
And somewhere between simple talks and shared silences,
Forever stopped feeling like an impossible thought."

Chapter III: The Vow

"Now he wishes not for journeys far away,
But for mornings where her smile begins his day,
To walk through life beside her, hand in hand —
Loving her always, in Ibadan, in Lagos, in every way."

The Forever Promise Oladipo Esther Oluwadamilola

I need you to know that you are my Valentine. Not just for now, but for every tomorrow, and for always.
